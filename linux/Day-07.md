## What is a process?
A process is any program that is currently running .
## Why does Linux give every process a PID?
it does , because names are not that unique , making PIDS better off.
## What is the difference between a foreground and background process?
foreground stops you from starting another process due to the current process running , while background process allows other processes to be ran while it continues uninterrupted .
## Why is top useful?
Top is useful to devops to find out processes that maybe frozen or eating up too much space in the computers CPU .
## What command would you use to stop a running process?
KILL PID's can be used to stop a running process .
